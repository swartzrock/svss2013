Silicon Valley
Scala Symposium

Friday, August 2nd, 2013 at Intel, Santa Clara, CA

Overview

The first annual FunConf Silicon Valley Scala Symposium will feature enlightening presentations by some of the best Scala developers in the San Francisco Bay Area. FunConf is a series of conferences on functional programming, data science, graph analysis, and other topics which make programming and data fun again! The symposium requires advance registration via SF Scala. Intel security will make plastic badges for the participants, so we need you registered with your full name matching your photo ID by EOD Thursday, 7/25.

Location

How To Get Here

The Silicon Valley Scala Symposium is held at the Intel campus, at the SC 12 building on 3600 Juliette Drive, Santa Clara, CA 95054. Directions: Exit 101on Montague Expressway. Turn left onto Mission College Blvd., while on Mission College Blvd., turn left on Juliette. Follow Juliette to almost the end of the street and there will be a sign to SC 12 on the left hand side. Intel employees and guests – pull into the SC 12 driveway.

Parking:

Once you enter SC 12, guests may park in any designated parking area. There are a very limited number of visitor spaces in the front left of the SC 12 building, there is also a large surface parking towards the rear left of SC 12 or you may park in garage “D”, which is directly behind the SC 12 building.

For registration, conference information or anything else please contact Alexy Khrabrov.
